# Case Study: Data Analytics For Red Bull - Sales Account

[📄 Download Full Project PDF](./red_bull_account_analysis.pdf)

## 📌 1. The Bottleneck (Problem)
[The variance in client accounts and why management needed to see which accounts were underperforming.]

## 🛠️ 2. Methodology & Tools
* **Analytics Software:** Advanced Microsoft Excel
* **Presentation Tool:** Microsoft PowerPoint
* **Core Metrics:** Account Sales Volume, Regional Distribution, Client Performance Variance

## 📊 3. Core Analysis & Findings
* **Visualized and isolated the best and worst-performing accounts**, delivering clear visibility into client behavior.
* **Identified the specific operational drivers** explaining why certain retail clients significantly outperformed others.
